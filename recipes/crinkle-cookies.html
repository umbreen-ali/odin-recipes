Mexican Chocolate Crinkle Cookies

Mexican chocolate crinkle cookies look fancy and taste delicious!

The powdered sugar coating on these cookies offer a crisp contrast to the fudgy inside texture. The combination of mocha, cinnamon, and cayenne is delightful!

Ingredients

  • 1 cup unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 1 ½ cup granulated sugar
  • ½ cup vegetable oil
  • 1 ½ tablespoons instant coffee granules (not coffee grounds)
  • 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • ⅛ teaspoon cayenne pepper
  • 2 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 tablespoon vanilla extract
  • 4 large eggs
  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 ½ cups powdered sugar

Instructions

  1. Using an electric mixer, beat the cocoa powder, sugar, oil, instant coffee granules, cinnamon, cayenne, baking poweder, and salt together until well combined.
  2. Beat in the vanilla and eggs.
  3. Slowly add in the flour. The dough will look like brownie batter.
  4. Refridgerate for 1-2 hours until firm enough to roll by hand.
  5. Roll the dough into 1-inch balls. Place each ball in a bowl of powdered sugar and coat throughly before placing on baking sheet. Space the balls at least three inches apart.
  6. Bake for 10-12 minutes. Cool completely on baking sheets before moving.
