Red Velvet Cake Mix Cookies

These cookies are easy and delicious!

If you're looking for an easy yet scrumptious cookie that will impress your guests, look no further than these cookies! The box cake mix keeps the ingredient list short, and the bright red color gives these cookies a festive look. White chocolate chips are added to the batter for a burst of sweet flavor.

Ingredients

  • 1 16.5 ounce box of red velvet cake mix
  • ⅓ cup vegetable oil
  • 2 large eggs, room temperature
  • 1 cup white chocolate chips

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F
  2. Line two large baking sheet with parchment paper or silicone baking mats and set aside.
  3. In a large mixing bowl, add cake mix, oil, and eggs. Mix until fully combined.
  4. Fold in the white chocolate chips until well combined.
  5. Scoop the cookie dough into balls of about 2 tablespoons in size onto the prepared baking sheets. Be sure to leave a little room between each one.
  6. Bake at 350°F for 10-12 minutes, until the tops of the cookies are set.
  7. Allow cookies to cool on the baking sheet for 5-10 minutes after removing them from the oven.
