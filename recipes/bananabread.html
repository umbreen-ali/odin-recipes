Banana Bread

Banana bread is better with chocolate!

This banana bread recipe is my absolute favorite! The base recipe is a classic banana bread recipe. The chocolate chips add a gooey decadance. Walnuts provide a bit of crunch to balance out all that ooey goodness.

Ingredients

  • ½ cup butter, softened
  • 2 eggs, beaten
  • 1 ½ cups all-purpose flour
  • ½ teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 3-4 ripe bananas, finely crushed
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • ½ teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1 cup of semisweet chocolate chips
  • ½ cup of chopped walnuts

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F.
  2. Cream together butter and sugar.
  3. Add eggs, crushed bananas, and vanilla extract.
  4. Combine well.
  5. Sift together flour, baking soda, and salt.
  6. Add sifted dry ingredients to creamed mixture.
  7. Mix until just combined. Do not overmix.
  8. Fold chocolate chips and walnuts into mixture.
  9. Pour into a greased and floured loaf pan.
  10. Bake at 350°F for 55 minutes.
